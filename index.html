Why We Exist

Millions Need Our Help

In a world of abundance, it's heartbreaking that millions still go to bed hungry, children miss school, and families lack basic shelter. Asha Foundation was born from a simple belief — everyone deserves a chance at a dignified life.

We don't just provide aid; we build pathways to self-reliance. Every rupee you donate goes directly toward transforming lives — not overheads.

What We Do

Fields We Transform

We work across four critical areas to create lasting change in the lives of the underprivileged.

📚

Education & Skill Development

Free coaching, digital literacy, vocational training, and scholarships to empower children and youth.

100+ Students Enrolled
🏥

Healthcare & Medical Aid

Free health camps, medicines, ambulance support, and surgeries for underprivileged families.

50+ Patients Treated
🤲

Social Welfare & Poverty Relief

Ration distribution, old age support, disability aid, and emergency cash transfers.

100+ Families Supported
🌾

Agriculture & Rural Development

Farmer training, organic farming, irrigation support, and seed distribution.

50+ Farmers Helped
👥

Youth & Community Development

Leadership camps, sports, mentorship, and community building initiatives.

100+ Youth Engaged
🎨

Arts, Culture & Heritage

Preserving local art forms, folk music, handicraft workshops, and cultural festivals.

25+Projects
🏘️

Shelter & Infrastructure Support

Building pucca homes, repairing slums, community toilets, and clean water systems.

100+ Homes Built/Renovated
💼

Training & Employment Programs

Skill development, resume building, job placement drives, and entrepreneurship support.

100+ Placed in Jobs
