Initiative

Education Access Program

We run after-school centers, distribute STEM kits, and sponsor 100+ students annually. Our curriculum focuses on holistic development, digital literacy, and career guidance to break intergenerational poverty.

Initiative

Community Feeding Network

Through 15+ community kitchens across Dhanbad and surrounding districts, we serve 5,000+ meals monthly. Our zero-waste model partners with local farmers to source fresh, nutritious ingredients.

Initiative

Rural Health & Checkup Camps

We mobilize medical volunteers quarterly to conduct free OPD, eye camps, dental checkups, and maternal health screenings. Telemedicine links are established for follow-up care in remote villages.
