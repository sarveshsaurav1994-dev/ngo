Shelter
🏗️ Safe Housing

Building Homes, Building Dreams

A home is more than four walls — it's safety, dignity, and hope. Ghar Ghar Umeed constructs pucca homes for families living in slums, under tin roofs, or on the streets. We provide weatherproof shelters with proper ventilation, sanitation, and electricity, transforming vulnerable living conditions into secure sanctuaries where children can study and families can thrive.

320+ Homes Built
1,600+ Lives Impacted
18 Communities
Renovation
🛠️ Home Improvement

Renovation & Repair Support

Not every family needs a new home — some just need help making their existing shelter safe and livable. We provide materials and labor for critical repairs: fixing leaking roofs, reinforcing weak walls, installing proper doors and windows, and improving sanitation facilities. These interventions prevent disasters and extend the life of homes for vulnerable families.

  • ✓ Roof Repair & Waterproofing
  • ✓ Wall Reinforcement
  • ✓ Sanitation Upgrades
  • ✓ Electrical Safety Installation
Amenities
⚡ Essential Facilities

Providing Basic Amenities

A house becomes a home when it has basic amenities. We ensure every family we support has access to clean drinking water, functional toilets, electricity connections, and cooking facilities. We also provide essential household items like beds, mats, and storage solutions. These fundamentals create an environment where health, education, and dignity can flourish.
