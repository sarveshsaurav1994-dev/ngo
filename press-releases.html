March 15, 2026

Crossing the 50,000 Lives Milestone

When Asha Vatslaya began in a modest rented space with just five volunteers, our vision was clear but our scale was uncertain. Today, thanks to relentless grassroots effort and unwavering donor trust, we've touched over 50,000 lives across 28 cities. This milestone isn't a finish line—it's proof that systemic change happens when communities unite. Our network of educators, medics, and volunteers continues to expand, ensuring no child is left behind and no family faces crisis alone.

February 2, 2026

Strategic Partnership with State Health Ministries

We're proud to announce a formal collaboration with the Jharkhand & Bihar Health Departments to scale our free medical camps to 10 additional rural districts. This partnership moves us from temporary relief to sustainable healthcare infrastructure. By integrating our mobile clinics with government health records, we ensure continuous care, maternal support, and early detection for over 15,000 underserved families. Healthcare is a right, not a privilege—and together, we're making it accessible.
